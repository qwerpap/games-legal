Bubble Bounce

Privacy Policy · Last updated July 19, 2026
Short version: Bubble Bounce does not collect or store any personal data itself. There are no accounts and no sign-in. The app shows non-personalized ads (Google AdMob) and uses anonymous usage analytics (AppMetrica) to improve the game. It does not track you across other apps or websites. Your game progress stays on your device.

Information we collect

None directly. Bubble Bounce does not ask for your name, email, phone number, location, or contacts. We do not require you to create an account or sign in, and we operate no servers of our own. Some anonymous, aggregated technical data is processed by the third-party SDKs described below.

On-device data

Your game progress, settings, statistics, coins, and unlocked items are stored locally on your device only. We cannot see this data. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Advertising — Google AdMob

Bubble Bounce displays ads through Google AdMob. Ads are requested in non-personalized mode: we do not use the advertising identifier to build a profile about you and the app does not track you across other apps or websites (we do not use Apple's App Tracking Transparency to request such permission). To serve and measure ads, Google may process limited technical data such as device model, IP address (for coarse geography and fraud prevention), and ad interaction data. This processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.

Analytics — AppMetrica

Bubble Bounce uses AppMetrica (provided by Yandex) to collect anonymous, aggregated usage statistics — such as app opens, level progress, crashes, device type, and OS version — so we can find bugs and improve the game. This data is not used to identify you personally and is not used for cross-app advertising tracking. Learn more in Yandex's Privacy Policy.

In-app purchases

Bubble Bounce is free with optional in-app purchases (such as Remove Ads or coin packs). All purchases are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see or store your payment details.

Children's privacy

Bubble Bounce is suitable for all ages. It does not knowingly collect personal data from anyone, including children under 13, and does not use behavioral ad targeting. Ads are non-personalized for all users.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
