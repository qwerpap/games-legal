About Arrow Shift

Arrow Shift is a free puzzle game for iPhone. No account needed and no personal data collected. The game is supported by non-personalized ads, with an optional one-time Remove Ads purchase.

Common questions

I lost my progress / changed phones. Game data is stored on your device. Reinstalling or switching devices starts a fresh game.

How do coins work? You earn coins by playing (or buy packs in the shop). They can be spent on hints, boosters, and themes inside the app.

I bought Remove Ads but still see ads. Tap "Restore Purchases" in the in-app Shop or Settings; make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID.

Something isn't working. Make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store, then email us with your device model and iOS version.
