About Plately

Plately is an AI meal-planning app for iPhone. It generates personalized meal plans and recipes, scans your food, and helps you hit your nutrition goals. No account is required — your profile stays on your device.

Common questions

How do subscriptions work? Plately Premium is an auto-renewable subscription (monthly or yearly) that unlocks unlimited AI generation and premium features. Manage or cancel it any time in Settings → your name → Subscriptions → Plately.

I subscribed but premium isn't active. Tap "Restore Purchases" on the paywall, and make sure you're signed into the same Apple ID you purchased with.

How do I cancel? Open the Settings app, tap your name, tap Subscriptions, choose Plately and tap Cancel Subscription. You keep access until the end of the current billing period.

The AI result looks wrong. AI-generated plans and calorie estimates can be inaccurate. Always double-check ingredients for allergens and treat the numbers as estimates, not medical advice.

Apple Health isn't syncing. Grant permission in the iOS Health app (Sharing → Apps → Plately), then reopen the app.

Something isn't working. Make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store, then email us with your device model and iOS version.
