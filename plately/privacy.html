Plately

Privacy Policy · Last updated July 4, 2026
Short version: Plately has no accounts and does not sell your data. Your profile and meal data live on your device. To generate meal plans, recipes and to analyze food photos, the relevant text and images are sent to our AI provider. Subscriptions are handled by Apple and our billing provider.

Information we process

  • Onboarding & profile data (goals, diet type, activity level, weight, height, allergies) — stored locally on your device and used to personalize your plans and calorie targets.
  • Recipe / meal-plan requests and food photos you choose to scan — sent to our AI provider to generate the requested result. Do not include personal or sensitive information you do not want processed this way.
  • Apple Health data (weight, height, activity) — only accessed if you grant permission, and used on your device to prefill or sync your metrics. It is not sold or shared for advertising.
  • Purchase & subscription data — a non-identifying transaction/receipt is processed to validate and manage your subscription.

Third-party services

  • OpenAI — processes your meal/recipe prompts and food images to generate results. See OpenAI's privacy policy.
  • Adapty — manages and validates in-app subscriptions and related analytics.
  • Apple — processes payments (App Store) and, if enabled, provides HealthKit data on your device.

What we do not do

We do not require an account, do not collect your name or email in the app, and do not sell your personal data. We do not use tracking across other apps and websites.

Data retention

Profile and generated content stay on your device until you delete the app or clear its data. Requests sent to third-party providers are handled under their respective retention policies.

Children

Plately is not directed to children under 13 and we do not knowingly collect data from them.

Your choices

You can revoke Apple Health access at any time in the iOS Health app, and delete all local data by removing the app. For any request, contact us below.
