Popsa

Privacy Policy · Last updated June 20, 2026
Short version: Popsa does not collect, store, or share any personal data. There are no accounts, no tracking, and no ads or analytics SDKs. Everything you do stays on your device.

Information we collect

None. Popsa does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal information. We do not require you to create an account or sign in.

On-device data

Your game progress, settings, statistics, and purchased cosmetic items are stored locally on your device only. We have no servers and cannot see this data. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Third parties

Popsa does not include third-party advertising or analytics SDKs, and does not send your data to anyone. The app works fully offline.

Children's privacy

Popsa is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
