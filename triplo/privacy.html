Triplo

Privacy Policy · Last updated June 21, 2026
Short version: Triplo stores your progress on your device. We do not ask for your name, email, or account. The only data leaving your device is what third-party advertising and purchase services need to show ads and process optional in-app purchases.

Data we collect ourselves

None directly. Triplo has no account, no login, and no personal profile. Your level progress, coins, and settings are saved locally on your device.

Advertising

Triplo is free and supported by ads served through Google AdMob. To show ads (including rewarded ads you choose to watch), AdMob may process a device advertising identifier and basic technical data. You can limit ad tracking in Settings → Privacy & Security → Tracking on your device. See Google's Privacy Policy.

In-app purchases

Optional purchases (such as Remove Ads and coin packs) are processed by Apple. Triplo never sees or stores your payment details — Apple handles the transaction.

Children

Triplo is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. Ads are configured for a general audience.

Your choices

You can reset ad tracking, decline App Tracking Transparency, or remove ads via the in-app purchase. Deleting the app removes all locally stored progress.
