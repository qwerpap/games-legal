About Loopa

Loopa is a free, offline puzzle game for iPhone. No account, no ads in the core experience, and no personal data collected.

Common questions

I lost my progress / changed phones. Game data is stored on your device. Reinstalling or switching devices starts a fresh game.

How do coins work? You earn coins by playing. They can be spent only on cosmetic themes inside the app — there are no real-money purchases.

Something isn't working. Make sure you're on the latest version from the App Store, then email us with your device model and iOS version.
