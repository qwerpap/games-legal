Floodo

Privacy Policy · Last updated July 2, 2026
Short version: Floodo does not collect or store any personal data itself. There are no accounts and no tracking. The app shows non-personalized ads served by Google AdMob; you can remove ads with a one-time purchase. Your game progress stays on your device.

Information we collect

None directly. Floodo does not ask for your name, email, location, contacts, or any other personal information. We do not require you to create an account or sign in, and we operate no servers of our own.

On-device data

Your game progress, settings, statistics, coins, and unlocked items are stored locally on your device only. We cannot see this data. If you delete the app, this data is removed with it.

Advertising

Floodo displays ads through Google AdMob. Ads are requested in non-personalized mode: we do not use the advertising identifier for tracking and the app does not track you across other apps or websites. To serve and measure ads, Google may process limited technical data (such as device model, IP address for coarse geography and fraud prevention, and ad interaction data). This processing is governed by Google's Privacy Policy. Purchasing Remove Ads in the in-app shop disables banner and interstitial ads; optional reward videos remain available and play only when you choose to watch them.

In-app purchases

Optional purchases (such as Remove Ads or coin packs) are processed entirely by Apple through the App Store. We never see your payment details.

Children's privacy

Floodo is suitable for all ages and does not knowingly collect any data from anyone, including children under 13.

Changes

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted on this page with a new date.
